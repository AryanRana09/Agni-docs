## Key Metrics & KPIs

At the top of your dashboard, you can quickly assess the health and performance of your voice agents in real time. 

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Total Minutes" icon="clock">
    The aggregate number of minutes consumed by all your agents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Agents" icon="users-robot">
    The total number of agents currently deployed and active.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live Calls" icon="phone-volume">
    The number of concurrent calls happening right now.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accrued Cost" icon="money-bill-wave">
    Your total usage cost, calculated at the flat $0.07/min rate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Success Rate" icon="bullseye">
    Goal completion metrics for your active campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversion" icon="chart-line-up">
    The percentage of calls resulting in a successful outcome.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Navigation Menu

The left sidebar is divided into three core sections to help you navigate your workspace logically:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Platform" icon="layer-group">
    Your daily operational views and data tracking.
    - **Dashboard:** Your high-level overview and metrics.
    - **Analytics:** Deep dive into usage, latency, and call outcomes.
    - **Calls:** Review call logs, transcripts, and recordings.
    - **Appointments:** Manage calendar events synced via Cal.com or GoHighLevel.
    - **Inbound Calls:** Configure routing for incoming traffic.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Management" icon="sliders">
    The core configuration of your voice AI brains.
    - **Agents:** Create, update, and deploy your AI agents.
    - **Tools:** Build custom functions and webhooks for your agents to execute during calls.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Operations" icon="briefcase">
    The backend infrastructure of your workspace.
    - **Phone Numbers:** Buy, import, and release phone numbers.
    - **Billing:** Manage your subscription and view invoices.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Activity & Trends

The dashboard also provides two essential widgets for auditing and forecasting:

- **Usage Trends:** An interactive chart visualizing your minute consumption over the week, month, or year.
- **Recent Activity:** A running log tracking organizational events (such as logins, account registrations, and agent updates) for security and auditing purposes.

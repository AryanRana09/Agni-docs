The **Phone Numbers** page is your control center for telephony. Before you can buy numbers or route inbound and outbound calls, you must configure your underlying telephony provider. Currently, Agni supports Twilio.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/telephony-strategy.png" alt="Choose Your Account Strategy" />
</Frame>

## First-Time Setup: Choose Your Strategy

When you access the Phone Numbers tab for the first time, you must choose how you want to manage your Twilio infrastructure. 

### Path A: Your Own Account (BYOT)
Choose this option if you already have a Twilio account and want full ownership, compliance control, and direct billing from Twilio.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Own Account">
    Click **Choose this option** under the "Your Own Account" card. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Selection">
    A confirmation modal will appear. Click **Continue Setup** to lock in your configuration.
    <Frame><img src="/images/telephony-byot-confirm.png" alt="Confirm BYOT Selection" /></Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide a **Friendly Name** for your integration, along with your Twilio **Account SID** and **Auth Token**. These can be found in your Twilio console. Click **Connect Account**.
    <Frame><img src="/images/telephony-byot-credentials.png" alt="Enter Twilio Credentials" /></Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Path B: Managed Account
Choose this option if you want a zero-configuration experience. Agni will manage the underlying infrastructure, and telephony usage will be deducted from your Agni credit balance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Managed Account">
    Click **Choose this option** under the "Managed Account" card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept Terms and Conditions">
    Because Agni handles the billing and compliance for Managed Accounts, you must review and accept the telephony terms, including the strict 28-day renewal policy and no-refund policy.
    <Frame><img src="/images/telephony-managed-terms.png" alt="Telephony Terms and Conditions" /></Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Friendly Name">
    Give your managed connection a **Friendly Name** to identify it in your workspace, then finalize the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Important:** The Managed Twilio selection is permanent for your workspace and cannot be changed later without administrative approval.
</Note>

## Managing Phone Numbers

Once your account strategy is configured, you will unlock the main Phone Numbers dashboard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/telephony-dashboard.png" alt="Phone Numbers Dashboard" />
</Frame>

### Buy a New Number
Use the left panel to search for and purchase new phone numbers:
1. Select your desired **Country** from the dropdown.
2. Use the search bar to filter for specific area codes or patterns.
3. Review the pricing (Outbound/Inbound per-minute rates and the flat monthly fee).
4. Select a number and click **Buy Number** to provision it immediately to your workspace.

### Owned Numbers
The right panel displays your active inventory. Any number listed here is ready to be used in your [Inbound Calls](/guides/inbound-calls) dispatch rules or as a Caller ID for outbound campaigns.

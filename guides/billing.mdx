The **Billing** page is where you manage the financial aspects of your Agni workspace. Because Agni operates on a high-performance, usage-based infrastructure (flat $0.07/min), your billing is managed through a combination of your baseline subscription and your prepaid credit balance.

## Available Plans

Agni offers dynamic subscription tiers based on your location. 
To view the plans available to your workspace, click the **Select region** dropdown in the top right corner of the **Available Plans** section. All subscriptions are processed securely via Stripe Checkout.

## Credits & Usage

Because real-time voice AI incurs immediate compute and telephony costs, Agni uses a prepaid credit system for call volume.

- **Available Credits:** This displays your organization's current, real-time credit balance. 
- **Buy Credits:** Click this button to top up your account. Your agents will consume these credits dynamically as they handle live calls. 

<Note>
  **Important:** If your credit balance reaches zero, your agents will be temporarily paused and active dispatch rules will fail to connect. Ensure you maintain a positive balance to avoid service interruptions.
</Note>

## Subscription Overview

This section displays the status of your currently active tier. 

To upgrade, downgrade, cancel your plan, or manage your payment methods (e.g., updating an expired credit card), click the **Portal** button. This will redirect you to your secure Stripe Customer Portal where you can safely manage your financial data.

## Billing History

At the bottom of the page, the **Billing History** table provides a transparent ledger of your past transactions. Once you purchase credits or subscribe to a plan, your official invoices and receipts will automatically populate here so you can easily download them for your accounting records.

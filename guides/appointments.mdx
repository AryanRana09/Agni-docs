The **Appointments** page acts as a centralized calendar for your Agni workspace. When your AI agents successfully negotiate a time and book a meeting with a caller, the resulting calendar events are automatically synced and displayed here.

## Integration Sources

Agni natively supports two major scheduling platforms. You can toggle between them using the **Source** menu on the left sidebar to view platform-specific bookings:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="GoHighLevel" icon="calendar-check" href="/api-reference/appointments/manage-ghl">
    View appointments synced from your connected GHL sub-accounts. Filter by **Confirmed**, **Cancelled**, or **Rescheduled**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cal.com" icon="link" href="/api-reference/appointments/manage-calcom">
    View appointments synced via your Cal.com webhooks. Filter by **Upcoming** or **Cancelled**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  **Connection Required:** If you see a "Failed to load appointments" error, you must first authenticate and connect your GHL or Cal.com account in your workspace settings.
</Note>

## Calendar Controls

To help you manage high volumes of automated bookings, the dashboard provides several organizational tools:

### Views & Navigation
- **View Toggles:** Switch between **Month**, **Week**, and **Day** views to adjust the density of your calendar timeline.
- **Refresh:** Click the **Refresh** button in the top right to manually pull the latest webhook data from your scheduling providers.

### Search & Filtering
- **Attendee Search:** Use the search bar in the left sidebar to instantly find bookings by the caller's name.
- **Status Filters:** Quickly isolate cancelled or rescheduled meetings to review the associated call logs and understand why the user changed their mind.

### Manual Actions
While agents handle the automation, you can still manually intervene. Click **+ New Appointment** in the top right corner to manually reserve a slot on the connected calendar directly from the Agni dashboard.

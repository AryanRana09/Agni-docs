## Performance KPIs

At the top of the Analytics page, four high-level metrics give you an immediate snapshot of your platform's performance based on your selected date range:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Calls" icon="phone">
    The absolute volume of inbound and outbound calls processed by your infrastructure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avg. Duration" icon="clock">
    The average length of your real-time conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Success Rate" icon="bullseye">
    The percentage of calls where the agent successfully completed its configured objective (e.g., booking an appointment or resolving a query).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Agents" icon="users">
    The number of unique AI agents that fielded calls during the selected period.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Visualization & Trends

Agni provides four interactive charts to help you visualize data trends and optimize your agents:

* **Usage Trend:** Tracks total voice minutes consumed over weekly or monthly intervals. Because Agni operates on a flat $0.07/min pricing model, this chart directly correlates to your platform spend.
* **Call Volume:** Visualizes the frequency of calls over your chosen time period, helping you identify peak traffic hours and concurrency demands.
* **Outcomes:** A comparative breakdown of successful versus failed calls, allowing you to quickly spot routing issues or prompt inefficiencies.
* **Agent Performance:** Compares success rates across different agent deployments. Use this to A/B test system prompts, voices, or tool configurations.

## Filtering and Exporting Data

In the top right corner of the dashboard, you can control the scope of your analytics:

1. **Date Range Picker:** Filter your data by predefined ranges (e.g., *Last 30 days*, *Last 7 days*) or select a custom timeframe.
2. **Export:** Click the **Export** button to download your current analytics view as a CSV file for ingestion into your CRM, billing software, or internal reporting tools.

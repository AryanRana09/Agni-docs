The **Analytics** page provides a deep dive into the operational health and effectiveness of your voice infrastructure. It allows you to monitor volume, track success rates, and export data for external reporting.

  <img src="/images/analytics-overview.png" alt="Agni Analytics Dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Performance KPIs

At the top of the Analytics page, four high-level metrics give you an immediate snapshot of your platform's performance based on your selected date range:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Calls" icon="phone">
    The absolute volume of inbound and outbound calls processed by your infrastructure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avg. Duration" icon="clock">
    The average length of your real-time conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Success Rate" icon="bullseye">
    The percentage of calls where the agent successfully completed its configured objective (e.g., booking an appointment or resolving a query).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Agents" icon="users">
    The number of unique AI agents that fielded calls during the selected period.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Visualization & Trends

Agni provides four interactive charts to help you visualize data trends and optimize your agents. These charts automatically update based on your selected timeframe.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Usage Trend" icon="chart-line">
    Tracks total voice minutes consumed over weekly or monthly intervals. Because Agni operates on a flat $0.07/min pricing model, this chart directly correlates to your platform spend.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Call Volume" icon="chart-bar">
    Visualizes the frequency of calls over your chosen time period, helping you identify peak traffic hours and concurrency demands.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outcomes" icon="chart-pie">
    A comparative breakdown of successful versus failed calls, allowing you to quickly spot routing issues or prompt inefficiencies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agent Performance" icon="ranking-star">
    Compares success rates across different agent deployments. Use this to A/B test system prompts, voices, or tool configurations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Filtering and Exporting Data

In the top right corner of the dashboard, you can control the scope of your analytics to generate custom reports:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter by Date">
    Use the **Date Range Picker** to filter your data by predefined ranges (e.g., *Last 30 days*, *Last 7 days*) or select a completely custom timeframe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export to CSV">
    Click the **Export** button to download your current analytics view as a CSV file. You can easily ingest this data into your CRM, billing software, or internal reporting tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

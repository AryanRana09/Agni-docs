---
title: "List Twilio Owned Numbers"
openapi: "GET /api/v1/twilio-owned-numbers"
description: "List all phone numbers in your connected Twilio account."
---

### Authorizations

#### Authorization

`string` header `required`

Use `Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`.

### Response

#### 200 application/json

#### success

`boolean` required

#### message

`string` required

#### data

`object[]` required

#### data[].sid

`string` required

#### data[].phone_number

`string` required

#### data[].friendly_name

`string`

#### data[].status

`string`

#### data[].date_created

`string`

ISO 8601 timestamp.

#### data[].voice

`boolean`

#### data[].sms

`boolean`

#### data[].mms

`boolean`
